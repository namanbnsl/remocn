Demo videos keep needing the same shot — a conversation typing itself out. Rather than ship one rigid chat component, this adds the pieces and lets you assemble the flow.

<ChangelogPreview name="message-bubble" />

### New components

- [Message Bubble](/docs/ui/components/message-bubble) and [Typing Indicator](/docs/ui/components/typing-indicator) — the atoms.
- [Chat Flow](/docs/ui/blocks/chat-flow), [iMessage Chat Flow](/docs/ui/blocks/imessage-chat-flow), and [Telegram Chat Flow](/docs/ui/blocks/telegram-chat-flow) — assembled conversations, each with the platform's own look.
- [GitHub Sponsors](/docs/social/github-sponsors) — the sponsors card, as a companion to the stars card.
