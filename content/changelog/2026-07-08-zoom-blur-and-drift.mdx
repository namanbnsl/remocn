A depth punch between scenes, a slow camera drift for anything that needs to breathe, and two components for the specific problem of replacing one word with another without a hard cut.

<ChangelogPreview name="zoom-blur" />

### New components

- [Zoom Blur](/docs/transitions/zoom-blur) — the outgoing scene scales past the viewer into blur while the incoming one resolves out of it. `rise` adds a vertical pickup to the move.
- [Drift](/docs/layout/drift) — a continuous, slow parallax on any child. The fix for static shots that feel dead.
- [Rolodex Flip](/docs/typography/rolodex-flip) — the old value flips away on a 3D hinge as the new one flips in.
- [Value Swap](/docs/typography/value-swap) — a shorter, flatter swap for numbers and labels that change often.
